# Time Left Logo

## Concept

The mark combines:

- a clock face to express countdown and remaining time
- an open progress arc to imply that time is running out
- a forward-leaning hand shape to create urgency and motion
- a warm progress accent to suggest pressure increasing as the deadline approaches

## Files

- `time-left-logo.svg`: full horizontal logo for README, website, and marketing
- `time-left-app-icon.svg`: simplified app icon mark for iPhone app branding

## Color Direction

- Deep navy: focus and seriousness
- Bright blue: clarity and momentum
- Warm orange: urgency and approaching deadline
