What Timeflies does

Timeflies helps you manage deadlines with countdowns, tasks, notes, and reminder plans on iPhone.

Troubleshooting

If reminders are not appearing, please check that notifications are enabled for Timeflies in iPhone Settings.

If a countdown looks incorrect, verify the selected deadline and your current device date and time settings.

Privacy

Your data is stored locally on your device in the current version of the app.
