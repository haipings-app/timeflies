# App Store Metadata Draft

## App Name

Timeflies - Deadline

## Subtitle

Countdowns, tasks, and reminders

## Promotional Text

Stay aware of every deadline with live countdowns, focused tasks, and smart reminders that turn pressure into steady progress.

## Keywords

countdown,deadline,tasks,reminder,focus,productivity,planner,timer,goals,time

## Description

Timeflies helps you stay aware of how much time remains for the things that matter most.

Create a countdown for any goal, deadline, exam, launch, trip, or personal milestone. Break it into clear tasks, track progress, and keep the remaining time visible so it is easier to act with focus instead of delay.

With Timeflies, you can:

- Create countdowns with a title, deadline, and color theme
- See time remaining in years, months, days, or seconds
- Break each countdown into actionable tasks
- Check off completed tasks and edit task notes
- Build reminder plans with local notifications
- Keep your progress stored locally on your iPhone

Timeflies is designed for people who want deadlines to feel real, visible, and manageable. Whether you are preparing for an exam, finishing an application, planning a launch, or working toward a personal goal, Timeflies helps turn pressure into steady progress.

## App Review Notes

- No login is required.
- Data is stored locally on device in the current version.
- Email and SMS screens are configuration placeholders only in this build; external provider delivery is not enabled yet.
- The app uses local notifications for reminder scheduling.
