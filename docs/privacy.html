PRIVACY POLICY

Timeflies - Deadline

Effective date: March 29, 2026

Overview

Timeflies is designed to help you track deadlines, tasks, and reminder plans. In the current version, your countdown and task data is stored locally on your iPhone.

Information we collect

We do not require account registration in the current version.

The app stores information you enter, such as countdown titles, deadlines, tasks, notes, and reminder settings, locally on your device.

How information is used

Your data is used to display countdowns, calculate time remaining, manage tasks, and schedule local notifications.

Sharing

We do not sell your personal information.

In the current version, app content is not uploaded to a remote server by default.

Notifications

If you enable reminders, the app uses Apple local notifications to alert you about upcoming deadlines.
